FAQ

Questions, answered

Is it really just one resume?＋
Yes — one profile, maintained in one place. You keep a single living record of your experience, and 1Resume generates a distinct, tailored resume from it for every job you apply to. You never fork versions or lose track of edits again.
Will the AI make things up about me?＋
No. 1Resume only selects and uses work experience you've already confirmed you have. It decides what's most relevant for a given role — it never invents experience.
Can I import my existing resumes?＋
Yes — import as many as you like. 1Resume smartly merges them into your one profile, and when two versions conflict (a different job title, a different date range), the app asks you to confirm which details are correct instead of guessing.
What if I'm missing a requirement for a job?＋
That's exactly what the experience gap review is for. Before your resume is created, 1Resume compares the job's requirements against your full story and flags where evidence is missing. Often you already have the experience — it just isn't written down yet. We prompt you to add it, then include it where it counts.
Is the PDF ATS-friendly?＋
Yes. Every resume exports as a cleanly structured PDF designed to parse correctly in applicant tracking systems, so your application is judged on your experience — not your formatting.
Is there a limit on how many resumes I can create?＋
Apply to as many roles as you like. Daily usage limits apply.
